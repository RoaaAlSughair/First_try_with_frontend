ABOUT US

Here at ForestMatters we do our best to reduce deforestation and restore natural habitat by planting trees and maintaining current forests, protecting our planet is a serious business and to insure that we all have a bright future we need your help to achieve our mission.

Read below for ways to support us.

  • Volunteer Program

  • Join as a volunteer and decide how you could help, we usually need volunteers for the physical work, digging planting and also we need people to organize events.


  • we need all the help we can get so if you want to be a volunteer and help in a different way we are happy to hear your suggestions.

  • Donations Program

  • We accept donations to support our continuous work, we have a lot of different expenses to cover and you as a doner you could pick which line of work you would like to support.


  • Every penny counts and is used for our cause, our transparency is important so we puplish detailed reports of our expenses all the time.
